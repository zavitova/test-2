I am Taj Tarsha

Play Video

I’m a digital startegist turned art director with years of experience at a full service digital agency

/WHY

Every day I get to wake up and be creative.I love working with dynamic, small, and cross-functional teams, and encountering new challenges across the digital landscape.

/HOW

I graduated from UW-Madison with a degree in economics and spent two years working as a technology consultant in Chicago. I left my job to pursue passion in fashion and PR by becoming the creative director of a menswear brand in new york city.

In 2015 I had the opportunity to move back to my home town of Chicago and contribute to the start up ecosystem of the city by working as an account executive a start up called Mabbly, and worked hard to transition onto the creative side as an art director.

/WHAT

I am passionate about a process called data-driven creative strategy. I believe all projects start with a strategic approach and can be executed most effectively via programmatic campaigns in digital.

I am a self taught designer who uses sketch and data insights to help transform brands. I am data-driven and reverse engineer everything that I do to achieve a common goal.
